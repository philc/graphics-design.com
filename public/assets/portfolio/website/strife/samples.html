00 MIDI
For a while now we have been doing a lot of work with MIDI. We often use them in creating music. For instance, lets say I wanted to use a piece of Michael Jackson's Human Nature for a track I was doing and in this situation I didn't want to sample. Well, what I would do is find the song in MIDI form and use the XP-50 ta find out how to play the piece. Then I could add it to my own song. So I have compiled a collection of MIDIs that I think may be interesting to listen to or to modify in songs. Enter MIDI Collection
