Links

These are sites that I personally reccomend, each of which has been extremely helpful to my personal design career.

Please consider linking to GDS!


DeviantART
A great social site for viewing artwork by a community of artists.

The GIMP
A free image editing program that runs on Linux, Mac and Windows.

Inkscape
The premiere open source vector editing software. It runs on Linux and Windows, and it's free to use.

Jimmac
One of my favorite designers. He's very active in the Linux desktop design community and creates very cool icons and wallpapers.
